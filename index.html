About Us

Doris Ritenour

Hair Stylist

Doris Ritenour is a licensed hairstylist for the last 18 years. She is a master hair colorist and stylist, and certified in brazilian blowout, keratin Japaenese hair straightening, and hair extensions. Doris believes that listening to a client's needs and educating them on how to maintain their style at home is just as important as the creative look she delivers.
